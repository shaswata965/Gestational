About Healthy Coping

It’s safe to say that becoming pregnant is one of the biggest changes that can happen in your life. Given the physiological and emotional changes, it’s normal to feel overwhelmed, especially if you have diabetes to manage while pregnant.

It’s normal to feel:

  • Guilt: many women feel embarrassed or guilty because they feel like their own food habits have caused the diabetes during pregnancy
  • Anger: it may bother you that you have to change your lifestyle through diet, monitoring and even medications
  • Fear: you may be overwhelmed about having diabetes and having to take medication to manage your blood sugar levels.

As feelings of guilt, fear and anger are normal, please remember that these lifestyle modifications are NECESSARY for your and your baby’s well-being.
