Stress

How will my delivery go?
Will I be able to handle all the pain?
What if there’s complications?

These are all questions that may be running through your head while you prepare for the delivery of your baby. We all know what it feels like to be stressed out. Bringing a child into this world is a big life change and it’s normal to feel overwhelmed at times.

Depending on how you deal with stress usually, stress can cause you to crave and eat unhealthy foods and see friends and family less. Think about the last time you were stressed and how you coped with it.

You may be stressed if you’re experiencing any of these:

Mood swings

Irritation

Anger

Feeling overwhelmed

Loneliness

Memory problems

Inability to concentrate

Aches and pains

Diarrhea or constipation

Nausea and dizziness

Eating more or less

Sleeping too much or too little
