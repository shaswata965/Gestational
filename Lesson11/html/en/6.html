Reframe Negative Thoughts

If you feel shock:

  • Talk about how you are feeling
  • Realize that what you are feeling is normal.
  • Focus on the positives.
  • Learn about the things that are within your power to manage

If you feel angry:

  • Realize it’s okay to feel angry or resentful. It helps to talk about these feelings with someone. Getting it out of your mind can help you take steps to move forward
  • Try not to let resentment overtake you. Focus on what you can control and feel proud of the positive efforts you have made, no matter how small

If you feel guilt or self-blame:

  • Realize that you can’t change the past, but you can learn from it. Think about practicing your healthy habits as a new mom and passing them on to your child

If you feel sadness or worry:

  • Realize that sadness and worry are normal emotions
  • Talk to a professional if feelings of sadness or hopelessness last a long time
  • Get involved in regular activity – One study showed that a 30 minute brisk walk was just as effective as antidepressant medication!
