Postpartum Depression

Postpartum depression is when women feel intense feelings of sadness, worry or anxiety that prevent them from completing their everyday tasks. This can happen a few weeks after childbirth.

Pregnant women with postpartum depression may cry for no reason, have trouble eating and sleeping and will feel overwhelmed by the sense of responsibility for taking care of a newborn child.
