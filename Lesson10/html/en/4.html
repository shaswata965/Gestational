Common Challenges to Eating Healthy

“I need to satisfy my pregnancy cravings.”

While some cravings may be an indication for a nutritional need, others can just be hormonal. Try healthier alternatives to your cravings, like Greek yogurt if you’re in the mood for ice cream.

“When I eat out there are no healthy options and everyone around me is eating unhealthy as well.”

It may be difficult to eat well while everyone around you is indulging. Check the menu ahead of time and consult with your health coach to see if there are options that could work for you. Explain to the people around you why it’s important to eat well for you and your baby.

“I don’t have enough time to change my eating behavior before the baby comes.”

Don't try to make too many changes at once. Small changes take less time, but they add up. Ask your family and friends for help as you change your eating behavior. This may involve having them help you to free up your time.
