Common Challenges to Staying Active

“My pregnancy is making me feel too tired to exercise.”

  • Exercise during pregnancy doesn’t have to exhaust you in order to work. A relaxing yoga or Pilates class is better than no exercise at all!
  • Also remember that physical activity is more than just exercise classes, it’s about moving around when you can, like taking the stairs instead of elevators, walking the long way to your car, getting up to talk to a co-worker instead of emailing them.
  • Try doing gentle, slow laps in a pool.
  • Have a light snack to get your energy up.

“I don’t have enough time.”

  • Break your 30 minutes a day into smaller chunks of three 10 minutes.
  • Park your car far from the place you want to go.
  • Take stairs instead of elevator.
  • Sweep or mop your floor briskly.
  • Stretch, do sit-ups, or pedal a stationary bike while you watch TV.
  • Plant and care for a vegetable or flower garden.
  • Take a brisk walk during your coffee or lunch break.
  • Ask a friend to go with you to take part in an exercise program at work or join a nearby gym

“I don’t have a car.”

  • You don’t need to go to the gym to get a good workout; try working out in your own home or area.

“I have physical limitations like joint problems, foot problems...”

  • Try stationary cycling, chair or other seated exercises, or water exercise
  • It is possible to do resistance exercises sitting down as well

“I don’t have child care.”

  • Be active with your kids.
  • Workout at home while watching your kids
  • Ask friends or family to help out.

“It’s too hot, cold, or wet outside.”

  • Work out indoors
  • Dress for the weather
  • Swim in hot weather

“It costs too much.”

  • Do free activities like walking.
  • Buy workout clothes and equipment on sale.
  • Look for free fitness classes at a gym or fitness center nearby.

“It’s boring.”

  • Try a dance class
  • Work out with a friend
  • Listen to music, watch TV, or talk on the phone while you are active
  • Keep trying new things until you find something you like
