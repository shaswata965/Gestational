Hypoglycemia Treatment: The Rule of 15

If you feel like you might have low blood sugar, follow “The Rule of 15”.

  • Step 1

    As soon as you suspect hypoglycemia, check your blood sugar level immediately.

  • Step 2

    If it is below 70 mg/dl, eat 15 g of fast acting carbohydrate immediately. Some examples are:

    • 3-4 glucose gel or tablets
    • Juice (150 ml)
    • Non-diet soft drink (150ml)
    • 3 teaspoons of sugar mixed with water

  • Step 3

    Wait 15 minutes, then check your blood sugar again. If you’re still below target, repeat step 2. If you’re within range, don’t eat or drink anymore sugar, even if you are still experiencing the symptoms.

  • Step 4

    Once your blood sugar is above 70 mg/dl (3.9 mmol/L) and you have recovered, take a long-acting carbohydrate of your choice, like 1 slice of whole-wheat bread, 1 small potato, ½ cup cooked oats, lentils or beans.
