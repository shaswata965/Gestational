Let’s Review!
  • When reading a food label, pay attention to the amount of carbohydrates, sodium and added sugars that are found in a single serving.
  • The ingredients on a food label are listed in order of highest quantity to lowest.
  • Added sugars can be in disguise in many different names: malt syrup, corn syrup etc.
  • Check with your doctor about meeting your nutrients requirements: Calcium, Vitamin D, Magnesium, Iron, Folic Acid (Folate)
