Finding Hidden Sugars

Many of the processed foods found at your nearest grocery store, even the savory ones, have sugar added to them. These sugars are added by the manufacturer or the cook.

You may be consuming more sugar from the box of biscuits that you’re snacking from than the sugar you add to your tea or coffee.

The popular honey, white sugar, brown sugar have all the same impact on blood sugars.

But also check food labels for the hidden sugars which will impact your diabetes management, just as much:

  • Agave Syrup
  • Beet Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Buttered Syrup
  • Cane Juice
  • Caramel Syrup
  • Corn Syrup
  • Date Sugar
  • Dextran
  • Dextrin
  • Dextrose
  • Diastase
  • Diastatic Malt
  • Ethyl Maltol
  • Fructose
  • Fruit Juice
  • Galactose

  • Lactose
