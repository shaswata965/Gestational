Preparing for Breastfeeding
  • Get informed. Talk to your doctor about breastfeeding with your own circumstance.
  • Don’t do it alone. Visit your nearest lactation clinic to see a lactation professional to help you learn about breastfeeding.
  • Prepare yourself mentally. Understand that like learning any other skill, breastfeeding requires practice and patience. Be prepared to face some challenges, like how to get your baby to latch onto your breast…
  • Get Support. Talk to your family members about your decision to breastfeed. Find a way to have your partner help in the feeding process
  • Plan ahead. Speak with moms who have breastfed and how they balanced it with work and family. Find out how they coped with challenges.
  • Get ready to go. Invest in nursing bras and button-down shirts or dresses to make feeding easier.
