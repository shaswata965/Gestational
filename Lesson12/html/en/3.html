Postpartum Care

Post-partum care usually consists of:

  • Breastfeeding (wait to learn about the endless benefits of breastfeeding for you and your baby!)
  • Follow-up tests with doctor 6-12 weeks after delivery
  • Balancing baby care with self-care to prevent or manage postpartum depression and stress
  • Continued healthy eating plan that includes vegetables, lean protein, whole-grains and other natural foods
  • Slow and sustained weight loss through healthy habits
  • Long-term follow-ups for reducing risk of type 2 diabetes
