Enjoy healthy carbohydrates

While all foods can affect your blood sugar, carbohydrates may have the biggest impact. A meal plan with consistent amounts of carbohydrates can help improve your blood sugar. This means that you eat the same amount of carbohydrates at each meal.

Examples of healthy carbohydrates are:

  • Starches (whole grain bread, brown rice, whole grain pasta)
  • Beans (lentils, chickpeas)
  • Starchy vegetables (winter squash, potatoes, corn, peas)
  • Fruit (apples, grapes, oranges)
  • Low-fat dairy products (milk, plain yogurt)

Foods that will not affect your blood sugar include:

  • Water
  • Non-starchy vegetables
  • Meat, chicken and fish
  • Oils, herbs and seasonings
  • Vinegar
