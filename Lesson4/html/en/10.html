Nutrients

Getting enough of the right vitamins and minerals is needed for the proper development of your baby. Missing important nutrients can lead to anemia, high blood pressure, complications during pregnancy, problems in proper fetal growth and even maternal death.

Please discuss with your doctor to ensure that you are getting enough of these nutrients. Here is a list of nutrients that you especially need during pregnancy:

  • Calcium
  • Vitamin D
  • Magnesium
  • Iron
  • Folic Acid (Folate) in the first 3 months
