Eat a Variety of Foods

You can continue using our guidelines to plan your meals:

At every meal, fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables.

A quarter of the plate can be filled with starches like brown rice, baked potatoes, and whole-wheat pasta.

In between meals, snack on fruits, unsalted nuts, and low-fat dairy.

Try to limit packaged, processed foods and drinks that are high in salt, saturated fat, and extra sugars.
