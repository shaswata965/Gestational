Potential Complications for Mom and Baby

Gestational diabetes increases your risk for developing a number of long-term, unhealthy outcomes such as type 2 diabetes and the increased risk of obesity and diabetes in the child.

For the mom, mismanaged diabetes can result in:

Pre-term labor or spontaneous miscarriage

Preeclampsia

Progressing to type 2 diabetes later in life

Retaining pregnancy weight

Hypertension (high blood pressure)

Postpartum depression

Your baby can also experience complications:

High birth weight (larger than average baby)

Hypoglycemia (low blood sugar)

Newborn Respiratory Distress Syndrome

Early birth

Birth injury

Still birth (death)
