Let’s Review
  • Self-monitoring is essential for managing diabetes for you and your baby’s benefit. Following your doctor’s monitoring plan will help you assess how you are managing your diabetes.
  • Your blood sugar target ranges and monitoring plan are going to differ from others, because everyone is different!
  • The procedure for monitoring is simple: wash hands, insert strip into glucometer, prick the side of your finger to take a drop of blood, place a drop of blood on the strip, read the glucose meter and immediately track your reading in Droobi.
