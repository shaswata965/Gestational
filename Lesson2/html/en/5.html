How to Test Blood Sugar

  • Wash your hands with soap and water, then dry them

  • Gather your glucose meter, testing strips, lancets and lancing device

  • Take a strip from the container

  • Insert the strip into your glucose meter

  • Put a new lancet into your lancet device every time

  • Prick the side of your finger to take a drop of blood

  • Apply the blood drop onto the correct part of your strip

  • Read the glucose meter and enter the reading into Droobi
