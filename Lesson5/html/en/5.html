Carbohydrate Portion Control

Because you can find carbohydrates in a variety of food groups (fruits, vegetables, dairy and grains), it’s important to think about how many servings of carbohydrates you are consuming in one sitting.

For Meals: 2-3 portions of carbohydrates
For Snacks: 1-2 portions of carbohydrates.

Starch 1 portion =

  • 1 slice whole-wheat bread
  • ¼ Arabic kubuz
  • ¼ Indian barata
  • 1 small chapatti
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened, dry cereal
  • 1/2 cup corn or bran flakes
  • 1/2 cup cooked: broad beans (foul), lentils, peas, white beans, oats, jareesh, harees, semolina, bulgur, freeka
  • 1/3 cup cooked pasta, rice, couscous, quinoa
  • ½ medium potato
  • 3 pieces medium size Falafel
  • 3 cups light popcorn
Fruit 1 portion =
  • 1 slice whole-wheat bread
  • ½ medium sized banana, mango or pomegranate
  • 2 medium sized tangerines or figs
  • 1 small chapatti
  • 4 medium sized apricot or plum
  • 3 medium sized fresh dates
  • 12 medium sized grapes or cherries
  • One cup melon, papaya, or pineapple
  • ¼ cup of dried fruits
Dairy 1 portion =
  • 1 cup fat-free, skim, low-fat milk
  • 1 cup skimmed laban drink
  • ¾ low-fat yogurt
