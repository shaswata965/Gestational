Protein and Fat

Eating protein alongside carbohydrates, or choosing carbohydrates that also have protein in them, helps to balance blood sugar levels. Fats have no carbohydrates at all.

Women with gestational diabetes should try to eat lean, protein-rich foods, such as:

  • fish, chicken, and turkey
  • eggs
  • beans
  • nuts
  • seeds
  • quinoa
  • legumes

Unsaturated fats are also part of a healthy diet. These kinds of fats promote your heart health and your babyâ€™s development.

Examples of unsaturated fats include:

  • olive oil
  • peanut oil
  • avocado
  • most nuts and seeds
  • salmon
  • sardines
  • tuna
  • chia seeds
